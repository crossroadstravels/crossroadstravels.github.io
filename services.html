CROSS ROADS TOURS SERVICES

Cross Roads Tours & Travel is one of Uganda’s leading tour operators and destination management companies, specialized in providing quality, individualized private and group tours within the region. We share our love for Africa by creating unforgettable travel experiences which reflect our care and respect for the destination and its people. With sustainability as a guiding principle at the core of our business, we develop creative and innovative products based on local knowledge and an understanding of the global market.

Our core business is designing and executing full-service tour packages for our clients, meeting your needs throughout the process and representing your brand on ground. Building meaningful and long-lasting partnerships is important to us, built on trust and our full support to provide you with all the tools and services required to boost your business and the tourism industry within our destinations.

CONCEPTUAL ITINERARIES

For those who want to be outstanding and offer our destinations beyond its touristic highlights, we can create conceptual itineraries that focus on a particular ideology, special interest or travel style.

Examples of conceptual itineraries are; all-female tours, built around supporting women empowerment initiatives and female tour guides; cultural adventures for immersive and responsibly developed encounters with local communities; active adventures for adrenaline-seeking travelers led by specialized adventure travel guides.

LODGE SELECTION

The Cross Roads Tours office is based in Kampala, which allows us to personally select the best accommodation options available within our destinations.

Regardless of the accommodations’ budget-range, a high service standard is our first priority to ensure a comfortable stay.

We prefer to work with small-scaled lodges in an intimate setting and in outstanding locations where possible, in order to provide the ultimate Africa experience.

GLOBAL UNDERSTANDING

With a culturally diverse team and experience since 2007, we have a global understanding of your clients’ needs and expectations, regardless of your source market.

This, along with our office being based in Uganda, allows us to at all times have up-to-date knowledge upon the best routings and logistics, select the best accommodation options available by personal experiences, continuously source for outstanding experiences, and have access to local talent to enhance your clients’ trip experience.

PRICING

We understand that all our partners are different, and that all offer tours to different audiences, from different source markets.

It ranges from individual clients who want to travel on a budget, to requiring the most exclusive lodge experiences, and from small to large join-in group tours, with or without private extension options.

We offer pricing solutions for all, ensuring that your tour remains both unique and competitive – always with value for money, always handled with the same level of professionalism and care.

EXECUTION

A seamless trip execution goes hand in hand with thorough planning, having the right facilitators on ground, using a reliable and well-maintained vehicle, but most of all having an extraordinary guide who will not only enhance the travel experience, but also take charge of the program by using his or her expertise, professional experience, social skills and network. As Cross Roads Tours & Travel we invest in our guides to utilize their full potential, and we trust them to execute your tour to the best of their ability. Our guides will not only reflect Cross Roads Tours & Travel’ values of quality execution, but they will fully represent your brand values too.

MULTILINGUAL GUIDES

When booking your tour with Cross Roads Tours & Travel we assure your tour will be executed by a highly experienced professional tour guide. We have English-speaking guides and German-speaking guides. Multilingual guides with knowledge of alternative languages can be offered on request depending on the travel time and availability of our guides.

SPECIALIST GUIDES

All our guides have versatile knowledge about the destination, parks and activities, culture and wildlife. May you require a guide with a specific specialty, such as a specialist birding guide or a guide with extensive knowledge on history, adventure or other specialties, always let us know so that we can make sure to make the right match for you.
