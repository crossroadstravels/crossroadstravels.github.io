About Us

Welcome to Cross Roads Tours & Travel Ltd. Cross Roads Tours & Travel Ltd is one of Uganda’s leading tour operators and destination management companies, specialized in providing quality, customized, private and group tours within the region.

We offer safaris and tours in the beautiful pearl of Africa, Uganda. Cross Roads Tours & Travel Ltd also offers learning, internships and research for students from overseas in Uganda! Our goal is to provide an enchanting experience; as said by Winston Churchill, “Uganda is the Pearl of Africa!” Tourism is a large part of Uganda’s foreign revenue and a big part of Uganda’s economy!
